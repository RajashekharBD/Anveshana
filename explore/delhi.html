About Delhi

Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Delhi is a land of breathtaking beauty, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth." With its snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, lush green valleys, and vibrant culture, Delhi offers an unforgettable travel experience. From the iconic Dal Lake with its houseboats to the majestic Gulmarg meadows and the spiritual Amarnath Cave, every corner of Delhi tells a story of natural splendor and rich heritage.

Why Visit Delhi?

Majestic Landscapes

Experience the stunning beauty of the Himalayas, with snow-covered peaks, alpine meadows, and crystal-clear lakes.

Houseboat Stays

Stay in traditional Delhii houseboats on Dal Lake, a unique experience blending luxury and local culture.

Adventure Sports

Enjoy skiing in Gulmarg, trekking in Pahalgam, and river rafting in Sonmarg - Delhi is an adventurer's paradise.

Culinary Delights

Savor authentic Delhii cuisine like Rogan Josh, Yakhni, and the famous Kahwa tea.

Handicrafts & Souvenirs

Take home exquisite Pashmina shawls, hand-knotted carpets, and intricate papier-mâché crafts.

Spiritual Journeys

Visit sacred sites like Amarnath Cave, Hazratbal Shrine, and Shankaracharya Temple.
